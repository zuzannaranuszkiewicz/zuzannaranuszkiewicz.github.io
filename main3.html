CASA V58

CASA V58

LOCATION: Vestergade 58, 8000 Aarhus

Wander around home to gathering points in the Aarhusian music scene in the 70s and 80s!

This vintage backyard is situated on the Vestergade, which holds an annual street party, where a red carpet is rolled out along the entire length of the street. It used to be the half-timbered farm and now is a home for places such as Venue 58, Trinbrædtet and Musikernes Hus. What are you waiting for?

Experience the place with other explorers’ eyes and get inspired!
